Privacy Policy

ImagineMyBaby · Last updated July 2026

ImagineMyBaby turns an ultrasound photo — and, if you choose, photos of two parents — into AI-generated baby portraits. Making those portraits requires sending your photos off your device to be processed, so this app is not a "no data leaves your phone" app. This policy explains exactly what we collect, where it goes, and how you can have it deleted.

No account, no name, no email

The app has no sign-up and no login. We never ask for your name, email address, phone number, or date of birth. To keep your orders and purchases connected across sessions, the app creates a random anonymous identifier (a "token") the first time you use it and stores it in your device's Keychain. This token is a string of random characters — it is not tied to your identity, and we cannot use it to learn who you are.

Photos you upload

When you create a portrait, the app sends the ultrasound image you select — and, for family portraits, the two parent photos you select — to our own backend service over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection. We use these images only to generate your baby portraits. The app reads only the specific images you choose; it does not access your wider photo library.

How your photos are processed

Our backend stores your uploaded images and the generated portraits with a third-party cloud storage provider (UploadThing) and sends your images to Google's Gemini image model, which generates the portraits from them. Google processes the images on our behalf to provide this feature. We do not sell your photos, use them for advertising, or use them to train our own models.

How long we keep your photos

We want to be honest here: we do not automatically delete your uploaded photos or the generated portraits. They remain stored on our systems so you can revisit your portraits and so we can support you. There is currently no automatic expiry.

You can ask us to delete your data — your uploaded photos and generated portraits — at any time by emailing support@dvoryashin.com. We will remove it from our systems. Because there is no account, please send your request from a device where you have your order open, or include the order reference shown in the app, so we can locate the right data.

Analytics

We use PostHog to understand how the app is used so we can improve it — for example, which screens are viewed and which steps of the flow people complete. These events are tied only to the random token described above, never to your name or email. We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA), we do not show ads, and we do not track you across other apps or websites. PostHog records general information such as app version, device type, and approximate location derived from your IP address as part of standard analytics.

Crash and error reporting

We use Sentry to capture crash reports and technical errors so we can fix bugs. These reports contain diagnostic information about the failure and your device; the app does not attach your token or any personal identity to them.

Purchases

Purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple processes your payment — we never see or receive your name, email, or payment details. To confirm a purchase, the app sends Apple's signed transaction receipt to our backend so we can unlock what you paid for. We record which purchases are active, tied only to your anonymous token.

Who we share data with

We share data only with the service providers that make the app work, and only for that purpose: UploadThing (image storage), Google (AI portrait generation), PostHog (analytics), Sentry (crash reporting), Apple (payments), and the providers who host our servers. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Children

ImagineMyBaby is intended for expecting and prospective parents — adults. It is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.

Contact

Questions or a deletion request? Email support@dvoryashin.com.
