ImagineMyBaby

ImagineMyBaby turns an ultrasound photo — and, if you like, photos of two parents — into AI-generated baby portraits. Here are answers to common questions.

How does it work?

Pick an ultrasound photo, follow the short setup steps, and the app generates a preview portrait. For family portraits you can add a photo of each parent. Generating portraits sends your photos to our service to be processed by AI — this needs an internet connection.

What's free vs. paid?

You can create a preview for free. Paid in-app purchases unlock the full results — such as a single portrait upgrade and the family portrait feature. Prices are shown in the app before you buy, and there is no subscription.

I bought something / reinstalled and don't see it.

Purchases are tied to your Apple ID. Reopen the app and your active purchases should restore automatically; if not, use the restore option on the purchase screen. If it still doesn't appear, email us with the order reference shown in the app.

Tips for the best portrait

Use a clear ultrasound image, and for parent photos use well-lit, front-facing photos where the face is clearly visible and unobstructed. AI results vary, and portraits are an entertainment novelty — an artistic interpretation, not a prediction of your baby's real appearance.

How do I get a refund?

Purchases are billed by Apple, so refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. If something went wrong generating your portrait, email us first — we're happy to help.

What happens to my photos?

Your uploaded photos are sent to our service and processed by AI to create your portraits. We don't ask for your name or email and don't use your photos for advertising. You can ask us to delete your photos and portraits at any time — see the Privacy Policy for full details.
