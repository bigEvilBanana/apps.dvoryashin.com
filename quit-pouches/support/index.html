ZeroPouch: Quit Nicotine Pouch

ZeroPouch helps you quit or cut down on nicotine pouches — tracking money saved, nicotine avoided, and your day-by-day progress. Here are answers to common questions.

How do I get started?

On first launch, pick "cold turkey" or "taper down," and enter your usual habit (strength, pouches per day, price per can). That's it — your numbers appear right away. You can change any of this later in Settings.

What's free vs. Pro?

Money saved with projections, nicotine-avoided stats, your projected quit date, the Craving SOS helper, your longest-streak record, progress charts, the craving journal with your #1 trigger, the home- and lock-screen widget, editable history, and the sourced recovery timeline are all free. A one-time $4.99 "Pro" unlock adds a step-down taper plan, pacing reminders, and your full forward recovery timeline. There is no subscription.

I bought Pro on another device / reinstalled.

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your one-time unlock is tied to your Apple ID.

Can I fix a day I logged wrong?

Yes. On the Progress tab, tap any day under "Recent days" and adjust the count. Your streak and totals recalculate — fixing a miscount never punishes your progress.

Is my data backed up or synced?

No. Everything is stored privately on your device only (no account, no cloud). A new device starts fresh. This keeps your data fully private.

Is this medical advice?

No. ZeroPouch is for general information and motivation only and is not medical advice. For free US support, call 1-800-QUIT-NOW or visit smokefree.gov.
