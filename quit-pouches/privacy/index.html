Privacy Policy

ZeroPouch: Quit Nicotine Pouch · Last updated July 2026

ZeroPouch does not collect any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no advertising. It makes no network requests and contains no third-party tracking SDKs.

Your data stays on your device

Everything you enter — your quit date, pouch counts, cravings, notes, taper plan, and settings — is stored only on your device. We never see it, transmit it, or have access to it. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it. You can also erase everything at any time from Settings → Delete all data.

Purchases

The optional one-time "Pro" unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store. ZeroPouch receives only whether the purchase is active (via Apple's on-device receipt) — no names, emails, or payment details ever reach us.

Notifications

If you turn on reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data leaves your phone.

Health & age

ZeroPouch is for general information and motivation only and is not medical advice. For free US support, call 1-800-QUIT-NOW or visit smokefree.gov.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
