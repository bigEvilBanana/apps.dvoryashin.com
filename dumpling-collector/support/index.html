Frequently asked

Are the values real prices?

They're point-in-time community estimates based on resale ranges, shown for reference only. They are not live quotes, offers, or appraisals, and real prices move constantly.

Where is my collection saved?

On your device only. The app is fully offline and has no account, so your data isn't backed up to us. Deleting the app removes its data.

A dumpling I have isn't in the catalog.

The catalog is a June 2026 snapshot and grows with updates. Email us the name and we'll consider it for the next catalog update.

Is this an official app?

No. It's an unofficial fan-made tracker, not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by RMS USA, Crazy Fun, Five Below, or any toy maker.
