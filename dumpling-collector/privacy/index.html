Privacy Policy

Dumpling Squishy Collector · Last updated July 2026

Dumpling Squishy Collector does not collect any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no advertising. It makes no network requests of its own and contains no third-party tracking SDKs.

Your data stays on your device

Everything you create in the app — the dumplings you mark as owned, duplicate counts, your wishlist, notes, and any photos you attach — is stored only on your device. We never see it, transmit it, or have access to it. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it.

Purchases

The optional one-time "Collector Pro" unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store. Dumpling Squishy Collector receives only whether the purchase is active (via Apple's on-device receipt) — no names, emails, or payment details ever reach us.

Photos

If you choose to attach a photo to an item, the app reads only the single image you select and stores it locally inside the app. It does not access your wider photo library, location data, or any other information.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
